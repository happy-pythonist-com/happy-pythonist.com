Frequently asked questions


Can I select specific sessions to attend ?

Unfortunately there is no such option available now. The reason is we strongly believe continuity is key in learning Python concepts, therefore we carefully prepared a clear course catalog that a learner enjoys. We intend our service makes you reach your goal by taking you throughout journey, but not a reference of knowledge. If you are looking to brush up random concepts by yourself, there are some great websites already like Udemy, Coursera.

How do I pay for sessions ?

We accept bank transfers or PayPal for payments. Please contact our managing administrator and they can guide you.

Do I have to pay everything upfront ?

No. We understand that learners and supporters have financial commitments. We accept monthly payments and you only pay for the sessions attended in that month. The hourly rate is applied for sessions and billed at the end of month to the user/supporter. You pay what you get, and we have no hidden tricks.
